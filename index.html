JLPT N5 Vocabulary — Minimal, Structured Cards

A JLPT N5 vocabulary deck with a structured, minimal card layout.
Front: kanji.
Back: kana reading, audio (most cards include audio; some use TTS), and color-coded pitch accent — Blue (Heiban), Red (Atamadaka), Yellow (Odaka), Green (Nakadaka).

Some cards include short grammar notes (e.g., “transitive verb”, “usually written in kana”). Meanings support multiple numbered senses. Example sentence and image fields are included but mostly empty so you can add your own.

Designed to render consistently across Anki Desktop, AnkiMobile, and AnkiDroid.
